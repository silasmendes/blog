Hello :)

I'm a Data Professional with a strong background in **Microsoft Fabric**, **Databricks**, and Azure data tools like **Synapse Analytics**, **Azure SQL**, and **Data Factory**. 

Lately, I've been diving deep into **Fabric Real-Time Intelligence** (RTI) and it has been really fun to learn more about streaming technologies, Kusto databases, and everything in between. On a daily basis, I write a lot of Python, KQL, and SQL to analyze data and troubleshoot issues. I'm also a big fan of modern open data formats like Delta Lake, Iceberg, and DuckDB, and I'm always exploring better ways to work with them.

Right now, I work as an **Embedded Escalation Engineer** (EEE). My main goal is to help support teams and customers resolve complex data issues faster. It is probably the most broad and dynamic role I have ever worked in because it covers so many different areas... and I absolutely love it! 

To give you an idea, my day-to-day usually involves:

* Jumping into complex support tickets to help unblock engineers and customers.
* Writing public documentation and self-help guides so users can easily understand the product and fix common problems.
* Spotting patterns in the issues customers face, and working directly with the engineering teams to improve the product.
* Creating and delivering training sessions for Support Engineers, and mentoring my team.
* Keeping an eye on support metrics to find new ways we can improve the overall customer experience.
